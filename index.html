Benefits of Parkour

  • Improves agility and flexibility
  • Enhances strength and conditioning
  • Boosts confidence and self-esteem
  • Encourages creativity in movement
  • Fosters a sense of community and support

Parkour Techniques

Mastering various techniques is essential for anyone interested in parkour. Here are some key techniques:

  1. Rolls for safe landings
  2. Vaults for overcoming obstacles
  3. Precision jumps for accuracy
  4. Wall runs for vertical movement
  5. Cat leaps for distance and height
